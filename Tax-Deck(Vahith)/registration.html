3. Difference between OPC and Sole proprietorship?

An OPC has unlimited liability which means the owner’s responsibility is limited to the capital he contributed and in case of a future loss, his personal assets are safe. Apart from that Sole proprietorship is usually preferred over OPC because of less regulations and ease of Formation.
