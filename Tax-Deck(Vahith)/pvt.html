Process

Share Documents

Our senior consultant will assist you with the name of the company, capital contribution and the documents required. All you need to do is drop us the documents over mail.

Register DSC and DIN of Directors

The next step is to apply for digital signatures and digital identification numbers (DIN). Our expert will apply for these on your behalf.

Company Name Registration

Now we would make a company name reservation with the government authority. So that your name is secured and no one can use it in the meantime.

Creation of company documents

It’s time to create the MOA and AOA of the company. It is meant for defining the purpose and internal governance of the company respectively. Object clause of the company would be created in order to suit your company requirements. Our consultants will assist you with all these documents.

Filing of form SPICe

Our expert will file the SPICe Form which is the final step in getting your Company Registration on MCA. Finally, you'll get a "Corporate Identity Number" (CIN) for your company.

GST Registration

We will get the company registered with the department. After that, our experts will file the GST application so that you can kick-start your company as soon as possible.

Key Considerations

  • Company Name

    Four (4) company names are to be decided in order of priority. This is where the company registration process.
    Expert Tip : Choose a unique name that does not exist in the MCA database and the trademark database.
    To check the name availability, kindly fill up the form on your right.

  • Authorised Capital

    This is the money you are authorised to invest into the company over a period. Moreover, the authorised capital can also be increased later.
    Expert Tip : Keep it between ₹ 1 lakh to 15 lakhs.
    Most startups keep it at ₹ 1 lakh because the purpose is to keep the registration costs low.

  • Paid up Capital

    Paid up capital is the amount that is to be deposited into company’s bank account. Moreover, this amount is to be invested in 6 months from the company registration date.
    Expert Tip :This should be kept according to the amount you’ll need initially.
    Minimum : ₹ 0
    Maximum : Up to your authorised capital.

  • Registered office of the company

    You can go for any of the options :

    1. Owned office space
    2. Owned house / apartment
    3. Rental office or apartment space
    4. Virtual office

    Note : You'll require a No Objection Certificate (NOC). We'll draft it for you.

  • When to start

    “You don’t have to be great to start, you have to start to be great”. The same is true for company registration. The sooner you start, the better..

What is the minimum amount of capital requirement?

There is no limit to the minimum capital requirement. To begin with, you can keep it to even Rs. 10,000/- only.

How many directors do I need for registration?

You require a minimum of 2 directors for registration. Most importantly, one of them should be an Indian resident. If you don't have a second director, then you can have someone in your family or friends. You can even allot just one share to the other director.

Do I need an office address for registration?

Yes, you need an address. However, if you do not already have an office, you can use your home as your office address. Furthermore, you can also change your address in the future without any hassle. In fact, 69% of TaxDeck Companies use their home as office address to begin with
