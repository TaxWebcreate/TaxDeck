things to know

Who is mandatorily required to apply for GST?

Business with a Turnover of 40 lakhs and above are required to register for GST. (10 Lakh in case of North Eastern and Hill states). Also certain businesses require you to register for GST mandatorily.

Does the person or entity having no GST registration able to collect GST?

No, the person who are registered under GST are only allowed to collect GST. The person who is not registered under GST is liable to claim input tax credit on the GST paid.

What is inter-state supply?

Inter-state supply of goods or service is identified to be the supply where location is a different state from the delivery location.

What is intra-state supply?

An intra-state supply of goods or service applies when the place of supply is within the same state as the location of the supplier.
