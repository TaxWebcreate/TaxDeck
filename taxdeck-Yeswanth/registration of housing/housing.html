Frequently asked questions

What is the role of society committee when Societyhive manages our society?

Society committee will continue to be the approver and meet regularly. Societyhive helps in planning and takes care of day-to-day tasks.

How do you maintain society register?

Managing Committee minute book. Register of General Body Meeting and special general body meeting.

What is the minimum number of promoters required to sign the registration proposal?

60% of the promoters who have joined the registration proposal are required to sign it.

What is the remedy available if the registration proposal is rejected?

Such rejection order can be challenged by filing the appeal under section 152 of the M.C.S. Act 1960 before the Divisional Joint Registrar C.S. Of the concerned Division

What fee is to be paid to the Government for registration of a Cooperative Housing Society?

Proposed society can be made the payment towards government fee as applicable from time to time. The present registration fee is Tenant Co-partnership /ownership CHS Rs.2500.00 Other (General) CHS Rs.2500.00
