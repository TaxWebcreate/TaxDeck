Startup India Registration

The Startup India Registration Scheme is a Central Government initiative to promote startups.

It helps startups avail a lot of benefits. These include fewer inspections, goodwill among customers (Recognised by Govt of India) etc.

It also acts as base registration to be eligible to apply for various government schemes. For example : Seed fund scheme for raising funds, income tax exemption for 3 years etc.
