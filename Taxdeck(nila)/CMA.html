Basics of LLP

A LLP has limited liability of partners which means the partner’s responsibility is limited to the capital he contributed and in case of a future loss, his personal assets are safe.

It is compulsory to file the annual return to “Ministry of Corporate Affairs” in case of a LLP.LLP is a form of Partnership Business in which the partners have limited liabilities.

A normal partnership can be started at around Rs. 1999 whereas a LLP can cost a minimum of Rs. 2300 Though the benefits of LLP way overpass the initial extra cost of starting
